    • BUOYANCY FORCE

    Bouyancy force is an upward force exerted by a fluid that opposes the weight of an immersed object.
    Achimedes' principle states that the upward buoyant force that is exerted on a body immersed in a fluid, whether fully or partially submerged, is equal to the weight of the fluid that the body displaces and acts in the upward direction at the centre of mass of the displaced fluid
