    The submarine is a vast transportation in the terms of underwater trans. They are developed. They are used mostly for military purposes and the war. They are difficult to detect by human eyes and they can attack from great distance by torpedos, their warheads. Navies invested on submarines, which they have bear the deadliest forms of destruction in World War 2. Such as the U-791 and the U-boat, and the USS Nautilus.

    The early technology of the submarines were undetected, Leonardo da Vinci design and made an invention of the submarine, but he knows that the submarine will be used for the war, so he kept the masterpiece in secrecy, untill the fellow Englishman William Bourne developed the early prototypes of the submarines in 1578; and other people like Magnus Pegelius that made the modern submarine, Cornelius Drebbel, in the year of 1801, Robert Fulton managed to create a Frence sub, with the power of human power. David Bushnel made the TURTLE which is a little submarine for one people forthe operation for bombing the British Ship. The sub was driven by Sergeant Ezra Lee. The operation failed, but the Sergeant Ezra Lee managed to escape. Untill a guy named Horace L. Hunley, created the sub we know today as the military sub, he took his own live while testing the submarine, but the sub shows a great opportunity for the Submarine world undewater.

    When the submarine was subjected to dive, they will open the water valve below the shipto let water current in. While water pour in the tank, the ship sank slowly because according to Archimedes, if the density of an object is bigger that the density of a liquid, the object will sink. As the water filled the tank, it will increase the density of the submarine, therefore, it sinks. But when the captain decide to rise it, a gas valve will open and the water valve will also be open. Massive air and gas, will push the water out of the ship and close the water valve, and the submarine will rise. The walls of submarine is design to withstand one of the strongest elements, the water. The hydrostatic pressure is a deadly game. Not just that they have to withstand the force of the current, switchback, undertow, tidal wave, temperture change, and even water vortex. The sub is interesting thing to study.

    Nowadays, the submarine was installed with a nuclear generator, and able to launch nuclear weapons. The submarines migth be the beast of war, but there is the submarine to find underwater artefact, for scientist reasearch, observation, leisure, and ven “submarines” in games.
